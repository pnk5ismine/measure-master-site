Key features

From photo perspective correction to curved line length, Measure Master focuses on the tools you actually use in the field.

Perspective correction
Fix distortion with a reference rectangle

Free-form shapes

About accuracy and limitations

Measure Master aims to bring the feel of ‚Äútape measure + field sense‚Äù into your phone. Please keep the following in mind.

  • It targets approximate values for decision-making, not certified engineering-grade measurements.
  • The more accurate your reference rectangle dimensions are, the more reliable the results will be.
  • Camera angle, photo quality, and lens distortion can all introduce error.
  • For legal or contractual measurements, you must still use proper professional tools and procedures.
